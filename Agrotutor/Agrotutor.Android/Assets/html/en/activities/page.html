Recording activities with AgroTutor

AgroTutor allows you to record all activities you perform in all your parcels. If costs are incurred, these can be added, and a total cost calculation is displayed in the main map page.

Below is a thematic list of the options included in the activities from AgroTutor:

Suitable varieties
• Suitable Varieties of Corn, Wheat and Barley
• Improved Criollos
• Improved varieties (includes open pollination (OPV) corn)
• Hybrid varieties (where previously they were not used or that are MasAgro Seedlings)
• Improved wheat varieties
Diagnostic tools
• Soil fertility (physical, chemical, biological)
• Diagnostic tools (soil and tissue analysis, rich strip, GreenSeeker, GreenSat, others)
Fertilization
• Soil fertility (physical, chemical, biological)
• Organic fertilizers as a source of nutrients (compost, manures, leachates, fermented organic fertilizers)
• Application of the fertilizer at appropriate times (with use of crop demand curves)
• Biofertilizers as a source of nutrients
• Land amendments: lime, dolomite and agricultural gypsum
• Way to apply fertilizer and optimize effectiveness: Buried manual, mechanical, yoke, superficial, foliar, fertigation
• Inorganic fertilizers as a source of nutrients (macronutrients and micronutrients) applied in the correct way and quantity.
Conservation Agriculture
• Coverage crops (canavalia, dolichos, mucuna, species of the region, sesbania, fodder peanut, etc.)
• Stubble management: use of manual or mechanical shredder to leave stubble on the surface
• Fertilizer-reformer applied to be able to do conservation agriculture
• Implements (discs, cutters, etc.) to adapt conventional machinery for use in conservation agriculture
• Multi-use / multi-crop machinery (in conservation agriculture system) for operations in zero or reduced tillage practices
• Crop rotation (with non-traditional crop)
• Vertical rotating with subsoil and chisel plow to prepare zero year
• Use of permanent beds
• Attachments adapted to 2 wheel tractor to prepare plot (subsoil and planer) to prepare zero year
• Inclusion of new crops in corn and small grain cereal production system such as: soy, triticale, sunflower, safflower, ...
Market and economy
• Incorporation of new high-value crops with market orientation
• Use of agriculture by contract
Post-harvest technologies
• Hermetic storage (bags, silos or plastic containers)
• Efficient sheller
• Grain drying
• Inert powders: micronized lime, diatomaceous earth and silicon
• Use of sex pheromones (field and storage control)
Other technologies
• Sustainable use of water
• Curves at level or level
• Leveling of floors
• Soil and water conservation works (filtering dams, live barriers, dykes, contreo or piezo
• Instruments for correction of compaction
• Leveling of the plot
• Sprinkler irrigation
• Drip irrigation
Integrated pest management
• Pheromones for pest control
• Beneficial insects
• Pest monitoring in the field (determination of economic threshold)
• Seed treatments (fungicides, insecticides, mycorrhizae)
• Use of functional biodiversity (natural enemies)
• Multi-use and multi-crop machinery (in a system different from conservation agriculture)
Soil conservation
• Milpa system interspersed with fruit trees (MIAF)
• Curves at Level
Topological fixes
• Optimal planting densities
• Row sowing for small grain
